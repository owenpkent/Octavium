## Pre-Production Checklist

- [ ] Charge camera battery

- [ ] Set up teleprompter with script loaded
- [ ] Open Octavium launcher on screen capture machine
- [ ] Have loopMIDI running with a soft piano VST ready
- [ ] Clean desk/background
- [ ] Good lighting (key light + fill)

## Camera Settings (Sony a7IV)

- **Resolution:** 4K 24fps (or 1080p 60fps for flexibility)
- **Picture Profile:** S-Log3 or HLG for grading headroom
- **Aperture:** f/2.8 - f/4 (shallow DOF for talking head)
- **ISO:** As needed for proper exposure
- **White Balance:** Manual, match to lighting
- **Focus:** Eye AF continuous

## Audio Settings (Zoom F3)

- **Sample Rate:** 48kHz
- **Bit Depth:** 32-bit float (no clipping!)
- **Gain:** Set for -12dB peaks on normal speech
- **Mic:** Lavalier or shotgun, your preference

## Screen Capture Settings (Streamlabs)

- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 or match to 4K
- **Frame Rate:** 60fps (smoother mouse movements)
- **Audio:** Capture system audio (piano VST output)
